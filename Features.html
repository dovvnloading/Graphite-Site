All Features

Canvas & Navigation

  • Node-graph canvas for spatial reasoning
  • Pan, zoom, and area zoom controls
  • Frames & Containers for organization
  • Conversation timeline minimap
  • Navigation pins and bookmarks
  • Sticky notes for annotations
  • Dynamic grid with snap, guides & orthogonal routing

  • Response regeneration
  • Scrollable and collapsible content

Connections & Relationships

  • Color-coded branches
Visualize relationships between nodes with customizable paths and connections that help clarify your reasoning flow.

AI & Analysis
